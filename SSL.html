Which SSL

Domain Validated (DV)

Padlock Icon in Minutes: Fastest way to protect your site

These encryption-only certificates are popular because of how fast and simple they are to get. All you have to do is prove you own the domain you wish to secure in order to get a DV certificate. And in just a few minutes you'll get industry standard encryption and trust indicators like HTTPS and the padlock icon. Whether a personal blog, micro-business, SME, or any business website, you should get a DV certificate, since it offers full encryption at the lowest price point. 

Organization Validated (OV)

Dynamic Site Seal: Business Assurance Validated SSL

OV certificates validate that a domain belongs to a registered business before being issued, and this process typically takes between 1-3 days to complete. The added benefit of an OV certificate is a dynamic site seal, which when clicked on, displays validated company information to a visitor, along with HTTPS and the padlock icon. These certificatesare perfect for registered businesses who do not have a budget for EV certificates yet.

Extended Validation (EV)

The Green Bar: The complete SSL solution

Extended Validation (EV) certificates are the most premium SSL solutions available. The main feature of EV certificates is the green address bar, the most universally trusted symbol across the web. Websites equipped with the lock and green address bar are instantly recognized as legitimate websites that can be trusted with personal information. The validation process typically takes a little more time, but if you have a registered business, we’ll help make sure you can get one!
